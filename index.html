About Us

At Donum Dei Hotel and Resort, we pride ourselves on providing unparalleled amenities to enhance your vacation experience. Located along the pristine shores of crystal-clear turquoise waters, our resort is a secluded paradise, providing a serene and exclusive retreat for our esteemed guests. From spacious oceanfront villas with private pools and breathtaking panoramic views to cozy beachfront bungalows enveloped by swaying palm trees, each room is meticulously designed to offer the utmost comfort and tranquility. Donum Dei Hotel and Resort is also the perfect destination for weddings, anniversaries, corporate retreats, and other special occasions. Our dedicated event planning team will ensure that every detail is meticulously arranged, creating unforgettable moments in an enchanting setting.

Services we offer:

Experience utmost comfort and relaxation in our well-appointed rooms and suites.

A delightful culinary experience at our on-site restaurant.

Unwind and rejuvenate with our recreational facilities and entertainment options.

Our resort provides versatile event spaces that can be tailored to your specific needs.

Discover the beauty and charm of the local area through our curated excursions and activities.

Our dedicated concierge team is at your service to cater to your every need.


At Donum Dei, we strive to provide exceptional service and a memorable experience for each and every guest. Let us be your home away from home, where relaxation, comfort, and warm hospitality meet to create unforgettable moments.
